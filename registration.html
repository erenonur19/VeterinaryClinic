About us

As Healers Veterinary Clinic Family:

Combining its 27 years of experience with a professional perspective, it works best with our animal friends by following and applying the technology, equipment and current treatments related to veterinarians and staff who work in harmony with each other, respect and love animals.

Our Medicine Principle:

It is the person who tries to work his best for animals by combining veterinary medicine education with his experience, with good will and love, and by adding his skill.

Our Mission:

To provide the best health care for all living creatures in order to distinguish between animals, owners or street animals or wildlife, according to equality and respect for living rights.

Our vision:

Being an exemplary clinic in our region with our innovative, principled and responsible approach.

Our slogan is:

Let's grow up by sharing love :-)
